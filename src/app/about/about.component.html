About Us

A few years ago we noticed that there was a severe lack of premium logs in the world. The ones that did exist were hard to come by and extremely expensive. So we set out with one goal in mind: To make premium logs affordable to everyone. We introduced or first log, the Logge Oak® to great success.

Since then we have expanded our operations immensely. We have opened a new brand in select test markets in Asia. And in China alone, we have sold more than 526 million logs this year thus far. But at our core we are still just the five guys who wanted to make the world a little better by offering premium logs to affordable prices.
