About Us

We bring logs into the future with our innovations

A few years ago we noticed that there was a severe lack of premium logs in the world. The once that did exist were hard to come by and extremely expensive. So we set out with one goal in mind: To make premium logs affordable to everyone. We introduced or first log, the Logge Oak® to great success.

Since then we have expanded immensely. Our products are being sold all over the world. We have opened a new brand in Asia. And in China alone, we sold more than 526 million logs last year. But at our core we are still just the five guys who wanted to make the world a little better by offering premium logs to affordable prices.
